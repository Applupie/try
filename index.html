About Me

I'm a graphic designer with a passion for graphic design, and am proficient in software such as Photoshop (PS), Illustrator (AI), Premiere Pro (PR), and After Effects (AE). I combine my creativity with technology to translate my imagination into visual work through these tools. Currently, I am studying Computer Art at Goldsmiths, University of London. At school, I have learnt several programming languages, which has provided me with more technical tools to create and refine my design work. Through these studies and practices, I continue to improve my technical skills in order to create even more impressive work.
